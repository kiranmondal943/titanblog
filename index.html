Frequently Asked Questions

Do I really pay $0 for hosting?

Yes. We utilize 'Static Site Architecture' which allows your site to be hosted on Enterprise CDNs (like Netlify/Vercel) within their generous free tiers for small businesses.

What about my Domain Name?

You pay that directly to the registrar (like GoDaddy or Namecheap). It usually costs ~$15/year. We do not mark this up.

Can I add a blog later?

Yes. The Titan Engine is scalable. We can add a blog, gallery, or more pages for a one-time expansion fee.

Is it secure?

It is safer than WordPress. Because there is no database to hack, your site is virtually impenetrable to common SQL injection attacks.
